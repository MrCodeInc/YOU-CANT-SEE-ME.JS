A simple javascript bookmarklet to hide almost all images on a webpage.

HOW TO INSTALL.

Copy the following code: javascript:(function(){var%20imgs=document.getElementsByTagName("img");for(var%20i=0;i<imgs.length;i++)imgs[i].style.visibility="hidden"}());(function()%7Balert%20(%22YOU%20CANT%20SEE%20ME%22)%7D)() and then make a new bookmark. HOW TO SHOW IMAGES AGAIN.

To show images again refresh the page that the images have been hidden on and images should be back.
